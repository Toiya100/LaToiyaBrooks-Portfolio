Featured Case Studies

1) Systemwide Workday Budgeting Implementation (Higher Education)

Context: A multi-institution university system needed consistent, reliable budget planning, reporting, and governance.

Challenge: Disparate processes and spreadsheets across 26 institutions created inefficiencies and errors.

Approach:

  • Established program governance, scoped integrations, and coordinated campus PMs.
  • Led change management, training, and rollout sequencing; standardized reporting.
  • Built executive dashboards for status, risks, and decision tracking.

Impact:

  • Streamlined budget reporting and reduced errors by 30% (systemwide).
  • Improved cycle visibility and campus adoption with standardized templates.
  • Accelerated issue resolution via a clear RAID and liaison model.
